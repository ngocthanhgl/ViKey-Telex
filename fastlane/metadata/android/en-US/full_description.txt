ViKey is a Vietnamese Telex keyboard for Android - a fork of FlorisBoard with a ground-up rewritten Telex engine.

Features:
- Algorithmic Telex engine (no JSON lookup tables)
- Syllable-based recomposition - every keystroke re-parses
- Full Vietnamese diphthong support
- Z undo (strip tones back to base form)
- WW→W undo built-in
- English fallback detection
- Gi/Qu syllable-aware handling
