<p><i>ViKey</i> is a Vietnamese Telex keyboard for Android — a fork of FlorisBoard with a ground-up rewritten Telex engine.</p>

<p><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
    <li>Algorithmic Telex engine (no JSON lookup tables)</li>
    <li>Syllable-based recomposition — every keystroke re-parses</li>
    <li>Full Vietnamese diphthong support</li>
    <li>Z undo (strip tones back to base form)</li>
    <li>WW→W undo built-in</li>
    <li>English fallback detection</li>
    <li>Gi/Qu syllable-aware handling</li>
</ul>
